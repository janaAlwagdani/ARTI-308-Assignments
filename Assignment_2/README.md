## Dataset and Machine Learning Problem Summary

The dataset contains 3,282 records of chocolate sales transactions, including details such as sales person, country, product type, date, sales amount, and number of boxes shipped. It represents real-world sales data across multiple regions and chocolate products.

The machine learning problem associated with this dataset can be formulated as a regression problem, where the goal is to predict the sales amount based on features such as product type, country, sales person, and boxes shipped. Alternatively, it can also be treated as a sales forecasting or business analytics problem to understand patterns, trends, and factors influencing chocolate sales performance.
